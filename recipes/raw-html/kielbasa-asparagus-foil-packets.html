Kielbasa & Asparagus Foil Packets

⏱ 25 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
360Calories
20gProtein
28gFat
6gNet Carbs

Sliced kielbasa, asparagus, and cherry tomatoes in foil packets with butter, garlic, and herbs. Grill or oven, 20 minutes, zero cleanup. The easiest meal in the rotation.

Ingredients
Packets
·1.5 lbs kielbasa, sliced
·1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
·1 cup cherry tomatoes
·4 tbsp butter, divided
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp Italian seasoning
·Salt and pepper
·Lemon slices
·Fresh parsley
Salad
·4 cups mixed greens
·Cherry tomatoes
·3 tbsp red wine vinaigrette
Instructions
1
Prep Packets
Preheat grill to medium-high or oven to 400°F. Cut 4 large sheets of heavy duty foil.
2
Fill Packets
Divide kielbasa, asparagus, and cherry tomatoes evenly among foil sheets. Top each with 1 tbsp butter, minced garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and a lemon slice.
3
Seal Tightly
Fold foil over contents and crimp edges tightly to seal. No steam should escape.
4
Cook
Grill over medium-high for 18-20 minutes, or bake in oven at 400°F for 20-22 minutes.
5
Open and Serve
Carefully open packets — steam will escape. Garnish with fresh parsley. Serve with side salad.

David's Notes

→ These are perfect for busy Fridays — absolute minimum effort and cleanup.
→ Heavy duty foil prevents punctures. If using regular foil, double wrap.
→ Open carefully — the steam is very hot.
