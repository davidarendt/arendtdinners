Ground Chicken Larb

⏱ 20 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
300Calories
34gProtein
14gFat
6gNet Carbs

Ground chicken cooked and tossed with fish sauce, lime juice, shallots, fresh mint, and cilantro. Served over shredded cabbage — light, herbaceous, and incredibly refreshing.

Ingredients
Larb
·1.5 lbs ground chicken
·3 shallots, thinly sliced
·2 tbsp fish sauce
·Juice of 2 limes
·1-2 Thai chilies or red pepper flakes
·1 tbsp avocado oil
·3 green onions, sliced
Herbs
·1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
·1/2 cup fresh cilantro
·1/4 cup fresh basil (optional)
Base
·3 cups shredded green cabbage
·Cucumber slices
·Lime wedges
Instructions
1
Cook Chicken
Heat avocado oil in a skillet over medium-high. Add ground chicken and cook, breaking apart, until cooked through — about 6 minutes. Season lightly with salt.
2
Season
Remove from heat. Add fish sauce, lime juice, shallots, chilies, and green onions. Toss to combine.
3
Add Herbs
Fold in fresh mint, cilantro, and basil. The residual heat will lightly wilt them.
4
Serve
Spoon over shredded cabbage. Serve with cucumber slices and lime wedges.

David's Notes

→ Fish sauce might smell strong but it's the backbone of this dish — don't reduce it.
→ For Teddy's portion, use soy sauce or coconut aminos instead of fish sauce.
→ This is one of the lightest meals in the rotation — great mid-week reset.
