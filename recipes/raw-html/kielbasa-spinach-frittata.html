Kielbasa & Spinach Frittata

⏱ 25 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
380Calories
26gProtein
28gFat
4gNet Carbs

Sliced kielbasa, wilted spinach, and feta in an oven-finished frittata. Start it on the stovetop, finish under the broiler for a golden top. Impressive and effortless.

Ingredients
Frittata
·1 lb kielbasa, sliced thin
·8 eggs
·3 cups fresh spinach
·4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
·1/4 cup heavy cream
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·1/2 onion, diced
·2 tbsp olive oil
·Salt and pepper
·Fresh herbs (parsley or chives)
Salad
·2 large tomatoes, sliced
·Fresh basil leaves
·Olive oil
·Salt and pepper
Instructions
1
Preheat Broiler
Preheat broiler to high.
2
Cook Base
Heat olive oil in a 10-inch oven-safe skillet over medium. Cook onion until soft — 3-4 minutes. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds. Add kielbasa slices and cook 2-3 minutes until lightly browned.
3
Wilt Spinach
Add spinach and cook until wilted. Spread everything evenly in the pan.
4
Add Eggs
Beat eggs with heavy cream, salt, and pepper. Pour over the kielbasa mixture. Scatter feta on top. Cook on stovetop over medium-low for 4-5 minutes until edges are set but center is still wobbly.
5
Broil
Transfer to broiler for 3-4 minutes until golden and puffed. Watch carefully.
6
Serve
Rest 3 minutes, then cut into wedges. Serve with sliced tomatoes, fresh basil, and a drizzle of olive oil.

David's Notes

→ Silicone handle cover or oven mitt — the handle gets very hot under the broiler.
→ Frittata is forgiving — any vegetables or leftovers can go in.
→ Leftovers are excellent cold or room temperature for lunch.
