Beef Short Rib Lettuce Wraps

⏱ 30 min 🍽 2 servings 🍷 David & Anne Only
640Calories
44gProtein
46gFat
7gNet Carbs

Braised or pan-seared short rib meat pulled and wrapped in butter lettuce with a soy-ginger glaze and sesame spinach salad. Deeply rich and satisfying.

Ingredients
Short Rib
·1.5 lbs boneless beef short ribs
·Salt and pepper
·1 tbsp avocado oil
Soy-Ginger Glaze
·3 tbsp soy sauce
·1 tbsp sesame oil
·1 inch fresh ginger, grated
·2 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp rice vinegar
·1/2 tsp chili garlic sauce
Wraps & Salad
·1 head butter lettuce
·Sesame seeds
·Green onions sliced
·3 cups baby spinach
·2 tbsp sesame oil
·1 tbsp soy sauce
·1 tsp sesame seeds
·1 clove garlic, minced
Instructions
1
Make Glaze
Whisk together soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, rice vinegar, and chili sauce.
2
Sear Short Ribs
Season short ribs with salt and pepper. Sear in avocado oil over high heat 4-5 minutes per side until deeply browned. Rest 5 minutes, then pull meat apart with forks.
3
Glaze Meat
Toss pulled meat with soy-ginger glaze. Return to pan briefly to caramelize.
4
Make Sesame Spinach
Wilt spinach briefly in a pan with sesame oil and garlic. Dress with soy sauce and sesame seeds.
5
Serve
Spoon glazed beef into butter lettuce cups. Top with green onions and sesame seeds. Serve with sesame spinach salad.

David's Notes

→ Short ribs have serious fat marbling — they're one of the best keto cuts.
→ For a longer cook, braise in the oven at 325°F for 2-3 hours — the meat falls apart and is even better.
→ David and Anne only — this is a rich, indulgent Thursday treat.
