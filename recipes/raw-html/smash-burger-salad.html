Smash Burger Salad

⏱ 20 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
480Calories
40gProtein
32gFat
6gNet Carbs

Ground beef smashed thin in a cast iron until the edges are lacy and crispy, then crumbled over iceberg lettuce with cheddar, pickles, mustard, and sugar-free Thousand Island. The burger experience minus the bun.

Ingredients
Smash Beef
·1.5 lbs ground beef (80/20)
·Salt and pepper
·4 slices American or cheddar cheese
·1 tbsp avocado oil
Salad
·1 large head iceberg lettuce, chopped
·1 cup dill pickle slices
·1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
·1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
·1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
Thousand Island (Sugar-Free)
·1/2 cup mayo
·3 tbsp sugar-free ketchup (or tomato paste + sweetener)
·2 tbsp relish (sugar-free)
·1 tbsp white vinegar
·Salt and pepper
Instructions
1
Make Dressing
Whisk together mayo, ketchup, relish, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Refrigerate until serving.
2
Form Patties
Divide ground beef into 8 loose balls — don't overwork the meat.
3
Smash and Sear
Heat cast iron over high heat until smoking. Add oil. Place a beef ball in pan and immediately smash as flat as possible with a spatula. Season with salt and pepper. Cook 2-3 minutes until edges are deeply browned and crispy. Add cheese on last 30 seconds.
4
Rest and Crumble
Remove and let rest 1 minute. Break into rough chunks.
5
Build Salad
Layer iceberg, tomatoes, pickles, red onion, and shredded cheddar in bowls or a large platter. Top with smash burger pieces.
6
Dress and Serve
Drizzle Thousand Island dressing over everything. Serve immediately.

David's Notes

→ The crispy edges from smashing are everything — use maximum heat and a heavy spatula.
→ American cheese melts better than cheddar for the classic burger experience.
→ This disappears fast — consider making extra patties.
