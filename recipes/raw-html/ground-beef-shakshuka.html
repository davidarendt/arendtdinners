Ground Beef Shakshuka

⏱ 25 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
410Calories
38gProtein
26gFat
9gNet Carbs

Ground beef nestled in a deeply spiced tomato and pepper sauce with eggs poached directly in the pan. One skillet, bold flavors, and an egg yolk that makes its own sauce.

Ingredients
Shakshuka
·1.5 lbs ground beef (80/20)
·1 can (28oz) crushed tomatoes
·2 bell peppers, diced
·1 medium onion, diced
·5 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp cumin
·1 tsp smoked paprika
·1/2 tsp coriander
·1/4 tsp cayenne (adjust to taste)
·2 tbsp olive oil
·Salt and pepper
·4-6 eggs
·Fresh parsley and cilantro
Instructions
1
Brown Beef
Heat olive oil in a large deep skillet. Brown ground beef, breaking apart, until cooked. Season with half the spices. Remove excess fat if needed.
2
Cook Aromatics
Push beef to sides. Add onion and peppers to center, cook 4-5 minutes until soft. Add garlic and remaining spices, cook 1 minute.
3
Add Tomatoes
Pour in crushed tomatoes. Stir everything together. Simmer 8-10 minutes until sauce thickens and deepens in color. Season with salt and pepper.
4
Add Eggs
Make 4-6 wells in the sauce with a spoon. Crack one egg into each well. Cover and cook 5-7 minutes until whites are set but yolks are still runny.
5
Serve
Bring the skillet directly to the table. Garnish with fresh parsley and cilantro. Serve immediately — the eggs continue cooking in the hot sauce.

David's Notes

→ Serve immediately once the eggs go in — they overcook quickly.
→ The sauce is even better the next day — make extra and reheat with a fresh egg poached in it.
→ Teddy loves this — the familiar egg and beef combo works well for kids.
