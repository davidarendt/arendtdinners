Kielbasa Stuffed Bell Peppers

⏱ 30 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
400Calories
22gProtein
28gFat
11gNet Carbs

Bell peppers stuffed with diced kielbasa, cauliflower rice, diced tomatoes, and melted cheddar. A complete meal in an edible bowl that the whole family loves.

Ingredients
Peppers
·4 large bell peppers, tops removed and seeded
·1.5 lbs kielbasa, diced small
·1 cup frozen cauliflower rice, cooked
·1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes, drained
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp Italian seasoning
·Salt and pepper
·1.5 cups shredded cheddar, divided
·1 tbsp olive oil
Salad
·4 cups mixed greens
·2 tbsp balsamic vinaigrette
Instructions
1
Preheat
Preheat oven to 375°F.
2
Make Filling
Heat olive oil in a skillet. Cook diced kielbasa until browned, about 4 minutes. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds. Add drained tomatoes, cooked cauliflower rice, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Stir and cook 2 minutes.
3
Stuff Peppers
Mix 1 cup cheese into the filling. Stuff each pepper generously. Top with remaining cheese.
4
Bake
Place stuffed peppers in a baking dish. Bake 25 minutes until peppers are tender and cheese is golden.
5
Serve
Rest 5 minutes. Serve with simple balsamic dressed greens.

David's Notes

→ Dicing the kielbasa small rather than slicing it distributes the flavor better throughout.
→ These keep great in the fridge for 3 days and reheat in the microwave perfectly.
→ Mix colors on the bell peppers for a more visual presentation.
