Thai-Style Ground Beef Lettuce Cups

⏱ 20 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
340Calories
34gProtein
18gFat
7gNet Carbs

Ground beef cooked with fish sauce, lime, lemongrass, and chili, tossed with fresh mint and cilantro, and served in crisp butter lettuce cups. Bright, fresh, and addictive.

Ingredients
Beef
·1.5 lbs ground beef (80/20)
·2 tbsp fish sauce
·Juice of 2 limes
·1 stalk lemongrass, minced (or 1 tsp paste)
·2 cloves garlic, minced
·1 inch fresh ginger, grated
·2-3 Thai chili or red chili flakes to taste
·1 tbsp avocado oil
·3 green onions, sliced
Herbs & Wraps
·1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
·1/2 cup fresh cilantro
·1 head butter lettuce, leaves separated
Slaw
·2 cups shredded cabbage
·Juice of 1 lime
·1 tsp sesame oil
·Pinch of salt
Instructions
1
Make Slaw
Toss shredded cabbage with lime juice, sesame oil, and salt. Set aside.
2
Brown Beef
Heat avocado oil in a skillet over medium-high. Add ground beef and cook, breaking apart, until cooked through — about 6-7 minutes. Drain excess fat.
3
Season
Add lemongrass, garlic, ginger, and chili. Cook 1 minute. Add fish sauce and lime juice. Stir and cook 1 more minute.
4
Finish
Remove from heat. Fold in green onions, fresh mint, and cilantro.
5
Serve
Spoon beef into butter lettuce cups. Top with slaw. Serve with extra lime wedges.

David's Notes

→ Fish sauce is funky on its own but completely transforms when cooked — don't skip it.
→ Teddy can skip the chili and fish sauce — coconut aminos works as a milder substitute.
→ Lemongrass paste from a tube works great if fresh isn't available.
