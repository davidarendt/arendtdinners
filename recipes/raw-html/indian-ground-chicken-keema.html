Indian-Spiced Ground Chicken (Keema)

⏱ 25 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
360Calories
38gProtein
18gFat
9gNet Carbs

Ground chicken simmered with onion, garlic, ginger, and warming spices in a fragrant tomato base. Served over cauliflower rice with cooling cucumber raita — a complete Indian feast.

Ingredients
Keema
·1.5 lbs ground chicken
·1 medium onion, diced
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·1 inch fresh ginger, grated
·1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
·1 tsp cumin
·1 tsp coriander
·1 tsp garam masala
·1/2 tsp turmeric
·1/4 tsp cayenne
·2 tbsp avocado oil
·Salt
·Fresh cilantro
Base
·1 bag (12 oz) frozen cauliflower rice
Raita
·1/2 cup full-fat Greek yogurt
·1/2 cup cucumber, diced
·1/4 tsp cumin
·Pinch of salt
·Fresh mint
Instructions
1
Make Raita
Combine Greek yogurt, cucumber, cumin, salt, and fresh mint. Refrigerate until serving.
2
Cook Cauliflower Rice
Cook per package and season with salt.
3
Brown Chicken
Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high. Add ground chicken and cook, breaking apart, until cooked — about 6 minutes.
4
Add Aromatics
Add onion and cook 3-4 minutes. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 minute. Add all spices, stir and cook 1 minute until fragrant.
5
Simmer
Add diced tomatoes. Stir to combine. Simmer 8-10 minutes until sauce thickens. Season with salt.
6
Serve
Serve over cauliflower rice. Top with fresh cilantro and a generous spoonful of raita.

David's Notes

→ Blooming the spices in the pan before adding tomatoes is key — it wakes them up.
→ Teddy tends to do well with Indian flavors — mild by default, add cayenne at the table for adults.
→ Double the raita batch — it's incredible on everything and keeps 3 days.
