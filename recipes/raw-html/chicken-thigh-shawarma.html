Chicken Thigh Shawarma

⏱ 25 min 🍽 4 servings 🍷 David & Anne Only
460Calories
42gProtein
28gFat
6gNet Carbs

Chicken thighs marinated in warm shawarma spices — turmeric, cumin, paprika, cinnamon — seared until deeply crispy and served over greens with a parsley-tomato salad and tahini.

Ingredients
Chicken
·2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
·1 tsp turmeric
·1 tsp cumin
·1 tsp smoked paprika
·1/2 tsp cinnamon
·1/2 tsp coriander
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·3 tbsp olive oil
·Juice of 1 lemon
·Salt and pepper
Salad & Sauce
·3 cups mixed greens
·1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
·1/2 cup fresh parsley, roughly chopped
·1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
·3 tbsp tahini
·2 tbsp lemon juice
·2 tbsp warm water
·1 clove garlic, minced
·Salt
Instructions
1
Marinate Chicken
Combine all spices with garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Coat chicken thighs thoroughly. Marinate at least 15 minutes (or overnight in the fridge).
2
Make Tahini Sauce
Whisk tahini, lemon juice, water, garlic, and salt until smooth. Add more water for thinner consistency.
3
Sear Chicken
Heat a large skillet over medium-high. Sear chicken thighs smooth side down for 5-6 minutes without moving until deeply browned. Flip and cook 4-5 more minutes until cooked through.
4
Rest and Slice
Let chicken rest 3 minutes, then slice thickly.
5
Build Plates
Arrange greens, tomatoes, parsley, and red onion. Top with sliced chicken. Drizzle tahini sauce generously over everything.

David's Notes

→ Overnight marinade makes a huge difference if you can plan ahead.
→ Chicken thighs are far more forgiving than breast — hard to overcook and stay juicy.
→ David and Anne only night — this is the kind of meal that makes keto feel effortless.
