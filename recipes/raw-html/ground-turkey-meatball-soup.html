Ground Turkey Meatball Soup

⏱ 30 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
330Calories
36gProtein
16gFat
7gNet Carbs

Turkey meatballs with parmesan and garlic simmered in a rich chicken broth with spinach, diced tomatoes, and zucchini. A warm, cozy finish to 6 weeks of excellent cooking.

Ingredients
Meatballs
·1.5 lbs ground turkey
·1/2 cup grated parmesan
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp Italian seasoning
·1 egg
·Salt and pepper
Soup
·5 cups chicken broth
·1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
·2 medium zucchini, diced
·3 cups fresh spinach
·1 medium onion, diced
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp Italian seasoning
·2 tbsp olive oil
·Salt and pepper
Serving
·Grated parmesan
·Fresh basil
·Parmesan crisps
Instructions
1
Make Meatballs
Combine turkey, parmesan, garlic, Italian seasoning, egg, salt, and pepper. Roll into 1-inch balls. Refrigerate while soup base cooks.
2
Build Soup
Heat olive oil in a large pot. Cook onion 3-4 minutes. Add garlic and Italian seasoning, cook 1 minute. Add broth and diced tomatoes. Bring to a simmer.
3
Add Meatballs
Gently add meatballs to simmering broth. Cook 12-15 minutes until cooked through.
4
Add Vegetables
Add zucchini and simmer 3-4 minutes. Add spinach and stir until wilted. Adjust seasoning.
5
Serve
Ladle into bowls. Top with grated parmesan and fresh basil. Serve with parmesan crisps.
