Italian Sausage & Roasted Tomato Skillet

⏱ 25 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
420Calories
24gProtein
32gFat
9gNet Carbs

Sliced Italian sausage with blistered cherry tomatoes, wilted spinach, garlic, and olive oil. Finished with fresh basil and parmesan — rustic Italian cooking at its best.

Ingredients
Skillet
·1.5 lbs Italian sausage (hot or mild), sliced
·2 cups cherry tomatoes
·4 cups fresh spinach
·5 cloves garlic, minced
·1/4 cup olive oil
·1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
·Salt and black pepper
·Fresh basil leaves
·1/2 cup shaved or grated parmesan
Caesar Salad
·2 heads romaine, chopped
·1/2 cup caesar dressing
·1/2 cup shaved parmesan
·Black pepper
Instructions
1
Sear Sausage
Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add sliced sausage and sear until browned on both sides — about 5-6 minutes. Remove and set aside.
2
Blister Tomatoes
Add remaining olive oil and cherry tomatoes to the same pan. Cook over high heat without stirring for 2-3 minutes until skins blister and begin to burst.
3
Add Garlic
Add garlic and red pepper flakes to the tomatoes. Cook 1 minute, stirring constantly.
4
Wilt Spinach
Add spinach in batches, stirring until wilted — about 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
5
Combine and Finish
Return sausage to the pan, toss everything together. Finish with fresh basil and parmesan. Serve with caesar salad.

David's Notes

→ Use hot Italian sausage if you want some heat, mild if Teddy is sensitive to spice.
→ The tomatoes releasing their juices creates an incredible natural sauce — let them blister properly.
→ This works beautifully over zoodles if you want extra volume.
