Ground Turkey Stuffed Zucchini Boats

⏱ 30 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
340Calories
32gProtein
18gFat
9gNet Carbs

Zucchini halves hollowed and filled with seasoned ground turkey, marinara, and melted mozzarella. A complete keto meal in an edible vessel.

Ingredients
Boats
·4 large zucchini, halved lengthwise
·1.5 lbs ground turkey
·1 cup low-sugar marinara sauce
·1 cup shredded mozzarella
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp Italian seasoning
·Salt and pepper
·2 tbsp olive oil
Salad
·2 heads romaine, chopped
·Caesar dressing
·Shaved parmesan
·Black pepper
Instructions
1
Preheat and Prep
Preheat oven to 375°F. Scoop out zucchini flesh with a spoon, leaving 1/2 inch shell. Chop the scooped flesh.
2
Cook Filling
Heat olive oil in a skillet. Cook ground turkey with garlic until browned. Add chopped zucchini flesh, marinara, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Cook 3-4 minutes.
3
Fill Boats
Place zucchini shells in a baking dish. Fill generously with turkey mixture. Top with shredded mozzarella.
4
Bake
Bake 20-22 minutes until zucchini is tender and cheese is bubbly and golden.
5
Serve
Rest 5 minutes. Serve with caesar salad on the side.

David's Notes

→ Check marinara labels — many have added sugar. Rao's is the gold standard for keto.
→ Teddy approved — familiar Italian flavors in a fun format.
→ Great for meal prep — make extra and reheat for lunch.
