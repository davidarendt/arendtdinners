Kielbasa & Sauerkraut Skillet

⏱ 20 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
350Calories
20gProtein
26gFat
7gNet Carbs

Pan-seared kielbasa with tangy sauerkraut, caramelized onions, and whole grain mustard over creamy cauliflower mash. Simple, hearty, and deeply satisfying.

Ingredients
Skillet
·1.5 lbs kielbasa, sliced
·2 cups sauerkraut, drained
·1 large onion, sliced
·2 tbsp whole grain mustard
·1 tbsp butter
·1 tbsp olive oil
·1/2 tsp caraway seeds (optional)
·Salt and pepper
Cauliflower Mash
·1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
·3 tbsp butter
·1/4 cup heavy cream
·2 cloves garlic, minced
·Salt and pepper
Instructions
1
Make Cauliflower Mash
Steam or boil cauliflower until very tender, about 10 minutes. Drain extremely well — squeeze out excess moisture. Blend or process with butter, cream, garlic, salt, and pepper until smooth.
2
Caramelize Onions
Heat olive oil and butter in a skillet over medium. Add sliced onion and cook slowly, stirring occasionally, for 10-12 minutes until golden and sweet.
3
Sear Kielbasa
Push onions to the side. Increase heat to medium-high and sear kielbasa slices until browned, about 2-3 minutes per side.
4
Add Sauerkraut
Add drained sauerkraut and caraway seeds to the pan. Toss everything together and cook 3-4 minutes until heated through and slightly caramelized.
5
Finish
Stir in whole grain mustard. Serve over cauliflower mash.

David's Notes

→ Squeezing moisture out of the cauliflower is the key to non-watery mash.
→ The cauliflower mash can be made ahead and reheated.
→ Rinsing the sauerkraut briefly makes it milder if Teddy finds it too tangy.
