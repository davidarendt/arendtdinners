Lamb Kofta with Roasted Vegetables

⏱ 30 min 🍽 4 servings 🍷 David & Anne Only
520Calories
34gProtein
38gFat
8gNet Carbs

Spiced ground lamb kofta grilled or broiled until charred outside and juicy within. Served with roasted zucchini, cauliflower, and a tahini drizzle over a fattoush-style salad.

Ingredients
Kofta
·1.5 lbs ground lamb
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp cumin
·1 tsp coriander
·1/2 tsp cinnamon
·1/2 tsp smoked paprika
·1/4 tsp cayenne
·1/2 cup fresh parsley, minced
·Salt and pepper
Roasted Vegetables
·2 medium zucchini, chunked
·2 cups cauliflower florets
·2 tbsp olive oil
·Salt, pepper, cumin
Salad & Sauce
·3 cups mixed greens or romaine
·1 cup cherry tomatoes
·1/2 cucumber, diced
·1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
·3 tbsp tahini
·2 tbsp lemon juice
·2 tbsp water
·1 clove garlic, minced
·Salt
Instructions
1
Roast Vegetables
Toss zucchini and cauliflower with olive oil, salt, pepper, and cumin. Roast at 425°F for 20-22 minutes until golden.
2
Make Kofta
Combine lamb with garlic, all spices, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix with your hands until just combined. Form into 8 oval patties or elongated shapes around skewers if using.
3
Make Tahini Sauce
Whisk together tahini, lemon juice, water, garlic, and salt until smooth. Add more water if too thick.
4
Grill or Broil
Grill kofta over high heat 3-4 minutes per side, or broil on a foil-lined sheet pan 4 inches from broiler for 4 minutes per side.
5
Build Salad
Toss greens, tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion together. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
6
Plate
Serve kofta over salad with roasted vegetables. Drizzle tahini sauce generously over everything.

David's Notes

→ Don't overmix the lamb — overworking it makes kofta tough.
→ Ground lamb from Walmart works great. If unavailable, half lamb half beef is excellent.
→ David and Anne only night — go big on the tahini sauce.
