Ground Turkey & Zucchini Fritters

⏱ 25 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
350Calories
34gProtein
20gFat
8gNet Carbs

Ground turkey and shredded zucchini formed into golden pan-fried patties with feta, dill, egg, and garlic. Served with a proper Greek salad — fresh, light, and satisfying.

Ingredients
Fritters
·1.5 lbs ground turkey
·2 medium zucchini, shredded
·3 oz feta cheese, crumbled
·2 eggs
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·2 tbsp fresh dill (or 1 tsp dried)
·1/2 tsp onion powder
·Salt and pepper
·2 tbsp olive oil for frying
Greek Salad
·2 cucumbers, chunked
·1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
·1/2 cup kalamata olives
·1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
·4 oz feta, cubed
·3 tbsp olive oil
·1 tbsp red wine vinegar
·1 tsp dried oregano
·Salt and pepper
Instructions
1
Drain Zucchini
Shred zucchini and squeeze out as much moisture as possible with a clean kitchen towel. This is essential — wet zucchini = falling apart fritters.
2
Make Fritter Mix
Combine turkey, drained zucchini, feta, eggs, garlic, dill, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until combined. Form into 8-10 patties.
3
Make Greek Salad
Combine cucumber, tomatoes, olives, red onion, and feta. Dress with olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
4
Pan Fry Fritters
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook fritters in batches 4-5 minutes per side until golden and cooked through. Don't move them too early — let them develop a crust first.
5
Serve
Serve fritters hot with Greek salad alongside.

David's Notes

→ Squeezing the zucchini is non-negotiable — it determines whether these hold together.
→ Make a double batch and freeze extras — they reheat perfectly in a pan or oven.
→ The Greek salad is substantial enough to carry the meal if you want to eat lighter.
