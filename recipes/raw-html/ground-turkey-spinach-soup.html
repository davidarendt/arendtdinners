Ground Turkey & Spinach Soup

⏱ 30 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
320Calories
32gProtein
14gFat
8gNet Carbs

Ground turkey simmered in seasoned chicken broth with spinach, diced tomatoes, garlic, and Italian herbs. Light, warming, and the perfect Sunday reset meal.

Ingredients
Soup
·1.5 lbs ground turkey
·4 cups chicken broth
·1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
·4 cups fresh spinach
·1 medium onion, diced
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp Italian seasoning
·1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
·2 tbsp olive oil
·Salt and pepper
Serving
·Parmesan crisps
·Extra parmesan for grating
Instructions
1
Brown Turkey
Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high. Add ground turkey and cook, breaking apart, until browned. Season with Italian seasoning, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
2
Cook Aromatics
Add onion and cook 3-4 minutes until softened. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
3
Add Liquids
Pour in chicken broth and diced tomatoes. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cook 10 minutes.
4
Add Spinach
Add fresh spinach and stir until wilted — about 2 minutes. Adjust seasoning.
5
Serve
Ladle into bowls. Top with grated parmesan and serve with parmesan crisps.

David's Notes

→ This is your Sunday reset meal — light enough to feel clean after any heavy weekend eating.
→ Add a parmesan rind to the broth while simmering for extra depth.
→ Keeps great in the fridge for 4 days — flavor improves overnight.
