Braised Lamb Shoulder (Moroccan-Style)

⏱ 3.5 hours 🍽 4 servings 🍷 David & Anne Only
580Calories
46gProtein
40gFat
8gNet Carbs

Lamb shoulder braised low and slow with ras el hanout, preserved lemon, olives, and chicken stock until it falls apart. Start it in the afternoon — by dinner it's effortless.

Ingredients
Lamb
·3-4 lbs bone-in lamb shoulder
·2 tbsp ras el hanout spice blend
·1 preserved lemon, skin only, minced (or zest of 1 lemon)
·1 cup Castelvetrano or green olives
·1 large onion, roughly chopped
·6 cloves garlic
·2 cups chicken stock
·1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes
·2 tbsp olive oil
·Salt and pepper
Cauliflower Couscous
·2 bags (12 oz each) frozen cauliflower rice
·2 tbsp butter
·1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
·1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
·Juice of 1 lemon
·Salt
Herb Salad
·2 cups fresh parsley
·1 cup fresh mint
·1/2 cup fresh cilantro
·1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
·3 tbsp olive oil
·2 tbsp lemon juice
·Salt
Instructions
1
Sear Lamb
Preheat oven to 325°F. Season lamb shoulder generously with ras el hanout, salt, and pepper. Sear in a dutch oven with olive oil over high heat, 4-5 minutes per side until deeply browned.
2
Build Braise
Remove lamb. Cook onion and garlic in same pot 3-4 minutes. Add chicken stock, tomatoes, preserved lemon, and olives. Stir and bring to a simmer.
3
Slow Braise
Return lamb to pot. Cover and braise in oven at 325°F for 2.5-3 hours until meat is completely tender and falling off the bone.
4
Make Cauliflower Couscous
Cook cauliflower rice and toss with butter, parsley, cilantro, lemon juice, and salt.
5
Make Herb Salad
Toss parsley, mint, cilantro, and red onion with olive oil, lemon juice, and salt.
6
Serve
Pull lamb into large pieces. Serve over cauliflower couscous with braising liquid spooned over top. Herb salad alongside.

David's Notes

→ This is the most time-intensive meal in the plan but almost entirely hands-off.
→ Start at 2pm for a 5:30-6pm dinner.
→ Ras el hanout is available at most grocery stores — if not, use a blend of cumin, coriander, cinnamon, turmeric, and paprika.
