Cajun Kielbasa & Zucchini Skillet

⏱ 20 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
360Calories
20gProtein
28gFat
7gNet Carbs

Smoky kielbasa seared with zucchini, bell peppers, and bold Cajun seasoning. Fast, one-pan, and deeply satisfying. The cast iron makes all the difference.

Ingredients
Skillet
·1.5 lbs kielbasa, sliced into rounds
·2 medium zucchini, sliced into half moons
·2 bell peppers, sliced
·1 medium onion, sliced
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·2 tbsp Cajun seasoning
·1 tbsp olive oil
·Juice of 1 lemon
·Fresh parsley
Base
·1 bag (12 oz) frozen cauliflower rice
·Salt and butter
Instructions
1
Cook Cauliflower Rice
Cook cauliflower rice per package. Season with salt and a pat of butter.
2
Sear Kielbasa
Heat olive oil in a large cast iron skillet over medium-high. Add kielbasa in a single layer and sear without stirring for 2-3 minutes until deeply browned. Flip and sear other side. Remove and set aside.
3
Cook Vegetables
In the same skillet, add onion and bell peppers. Cook 3-4 minutes until softened. Add garlic, cook 1 minute.
4
Add Zucchini
Add zucchini and Cajun seasoning. Toss everything together and cook 3-4 more minutes until zucchini is tender but still has bite.
5
Finish
Return kielbasa to the pan. Squeeze lemon over everything and toss. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve over cauliflower rice.

David's Notes

→ The key is searing the kielbasa properly — high heat, don't move it, let it get a crust.
→ Cajun seasoning varies in salt content — taste before adding extra salt.
→ Add a splash of hot sauce at the table if you want more heat.
