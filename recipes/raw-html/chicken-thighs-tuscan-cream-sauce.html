Chicken Thighs in Creamy Tuscan Sauce

⏱ 25 min 🍽 4 servings 🍷 David & Anne Only
540Calories
44gProtein
36gFat
7gNet Carbs

Chicken thighs seared until golden then simmered in a sun-dried tomato, garlic, spinach, and cream sauce. One skillet, restaurant quality, 25 minutes.

Ingredients
Chicken
·2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
·Salt and pepper
·1 tbsp olive oil
Tuscan Sauce
·5 cloves garlic, minced
·1/3 cup sun-dried tomatoes in oil, sliced
·1 cup heavy cream
·1/2 cup chicken broth
·3 cups fresh spinach
·1/2 cup grated parmesan
·1 tsp Italian seasoning
·1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
·Salt and pepper
Sides
·1 lb broccolini
·2 tbsp olive oil
·Salt
·4 cups mixed greens
·2 tbsp lemon vinaigrette
Instructions
1
Sear Chicken
Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Sear 5-6 minutes per side until golden and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
2
Make Sauce
In the same pan, add garlic and cook 30 seconds. Add sun-dried tomatoes and Italian seasoning, cook 1 minute. Add chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits.
3
Add Cream
Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Cook 3-4 minutes until slightly thickened. Stir in parmesan and red pepper flakes.
4
Add Spinach
Add spinach and stir until wilted — about 1 minute. Season with salt and pepper.
5
Return Chicken
Return chicken to pan and coat in sauce. Simmer 2 more minutes.
6
Serve
Serve alongside roasted broccolini and dressed greens.

David's Notes

→ Use the oil from the sun-dried tomato jar to sear the chicken for extra flavor.
→ This sauce is so good you'll want to drink it — make extra.
→ David and Anne only night — this is a genuinely luxurious weeknight meal.
