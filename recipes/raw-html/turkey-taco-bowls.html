Turkey Taco Bowls

⏱ 25 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
390Calories
36gProtein
22gFat
10gNet Carbs

Seasoned ground turkey over cauliflower rice loaded with all the taco fixings — shredded cheese, sour cream, avocado, and pico de gallo. All the flavor, none of the tortilla.

Ingredients
Turkey
·1.5 lbs ground turkey
·1 tsp cumin
·1 tsp chili powder
·1/2 tsp smoked paprika
·1/2 tsp garlic powder
·1/2 tsp onion powder
·Salt and pepper
·1 tbsp olive oil
·2 tbsp water
Base & Toppings
·1 bag (12 oz) frozen cauliflower rice
·1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
·1/2 cup sour cream
·2 avocados, diced
·1 cup pico de gallo (fresh or store-bought)
Slaw
·2 cups shredded cabbage
·Juice of 1 lime
·Pinch of salt
·1 tbsp olive oil
Instructions
1
Cook Cauliflower Rice
Cook cauliflower rice per package. Season with salt and set aside.
2
Brown Turkey
Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high. Add ground turkey and cook, breaking apart, until no longer pink — about 6-7 minutes.
3
Season
Add cumin, chili powder, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, and 2 tbsp water. Stir and cook 2 more minutes until fragrant and well coated.
4
Make Slaw
Toss shredded cabbage with lime juice, olive oil, and a pinch of salt.
5
Build Bowls
Add cauliflower rice to bowls. Top with seasoned turkey, shredded cheese, sour cream, diced avocado, and pico de gallo. Serve slaw alongside.

David's Notes

→ Ground turkey can be dry — don't overcook it. Pull it off heat as soon as it's no longer pink.
→ Teddy loves this one. The familiar taco flavors are a big hit.
→ Make a double batch of the seasoned turkey — it reheats perfectly for next-day lunches.
