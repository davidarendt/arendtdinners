Kielbasa & Cauliflower Fried Rice

⏱ 20 min 🍽 4 servings 👦 Teddy Approved
370Calories
22gProtein
26gFat
8gNet Carbs

Riced cauliflower stir-fried with diced kielbasa, eggs, soy sauce, sesame oil, and scallions. Tastes like your favorite takeout fried rice but strict keto.

Ingredients
Fried Rice
·1.5 lbs kielbasa, diced small
·2 bags (12oz each) frozen cauliflower rice
·4 eggs, beaten
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·1 inch fresh ginger, grated
·3 tbsp soy sauce (or coconut aminos)
·2 tbsp sesame oil
·1 tbsp avocado oil
·4 green onions, sliced
·1 cup frozen peas (optional)
Cucumber Salad
·2 cucumbers, sliced
·2 tbsp rice vinegar
·1 tbsp sesame oil
·1 tsp sesame seeds
·Pinch of salt
Instructions
1
Make Cucumber Salad
Combine cucumber slices with rice vinegar, sesame oil, sesame seeds, and salt. Set aside.
2
Cook Cauliflower Rice
Cook cauliflower rice per package. Spread on a baking sheet or paper towel to dry out slightly — this prevents a soggy stir fry.
3
Brown Kielbasa
Heat avocado oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat. Add diced kielbasa and cook until browned — about 4 minutes. Remove and set aside.
4
Scramble Eggs
In the same pan, pour in beaten eggs. Scramble lightly and break into pieces — don't fully cook. Remove.
5
Stir Fry
Add a little more oil if needed. Add garlic and ginger, 30 seconds. Add dried cauliflower rice and stir fry 3-4 minutes over high heat.
6
Combine
Return kielbasa and egg to pan. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, peas if using, and green onions. Toss everything together over high heat for 1-2 minutes.

David's Notes

→ High heat is essential for proper stir fry — don't be afraid of the flame.
→ The drying step for cauliflower rice makes a massive difference in texture.
→ This is one of the most family-friendly meals in the rotation.
