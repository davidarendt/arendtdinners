# Greek Ground Beef Skillet

![Greek Ground Beef Skillet](https://closetcooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Greek-Beef-Skillet-1200-2.jpg)

## Ingredients

### Main Skillet

- 1.5 lbs ground beef (80/20)
- 3 cups fresh spinach
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/2 cup kalamata olives, halved
- 4 oz crumbled feta cheese
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon, juiced
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- Fresh parsley, for garnish

### Base

- 1 bag (12 oz) frozen cauliflower rice

### Tzatziki Side (optional)

- 1/2 cup full-fat Greek yogurt
- 1 cup cucumber, finely diced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- Pinch of salt
- Fresh dill (optional)

## Instructions

1. Cook frozen cauliflower rice according to package directions. Season with a little salt and a drizzle of olive oil. Set aside and keep warm.
2. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add ground beef and break it apart while cooking. Season with oregano, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook until browned, about 7 to 8 minutes. Drain excess fat if needed.
3. Push beef to one side of the skillet. Add remaining olive oil and garlic to the empty side. Cook for 30 seconds until fragrant, then stir into the beef.
4. Add cherry tomatoes and kalamata olives. Stir and cook 2 to 3 minutes, until tomatoes begin to blister.
5. Add spinach in handfuls and stir until wilted, about 2 minutes. Squeeze half the lemon over the skillet and stir to combine.
6. Serve beef mixture over cauliflower rice. Top with crumbled feta, remaining lemon juice, and fresh parsley.
7. Optional side: mix Greek yogurt, cucumber, garlic, salt, and dill to make a quick tzatziki.

## Notes

- 80/20 beef keeps the dish rich and keto-friendly; drain only excess fat.
- Leftovers reheat well and are good for meal prep.
